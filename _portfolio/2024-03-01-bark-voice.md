## Project Overview

**Bark Voice Cloning** is an all-in-one Text-to-Speech (TTS) toolkit that unifies multiple mainstream models into one-click training and inference scripts. It features an easy-to-use **Gradio UI** for rapid voice cloning across English and Chinese speech.

### Key Achievements
*   **3,000+ Stars** on GitHub.
*   Top 20 application on Hugging Face (sorted by trending).
*   Featured in *South Reviews* (Chinese politics/economics magazine).
*   Showcased in a live stream by *Himalaya* with ~1,000 viewers.
