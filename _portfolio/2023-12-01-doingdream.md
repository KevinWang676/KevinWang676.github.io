## Project Overview

**DoingDream AI** is an all-in-one platform offering multimodal AI services, including voice cloning, voice conversion, and music generation.

### Key Achievements
*   **Founder & Full-Stack Developer**: Built the platform from scratch.
*   **User Growth**: Rapidly attracted over **19,000 registered users** and **1,000+ monthly paying users**.
*   **Awards**: Won **2nd Place** at the 2nd Midwest Chinese Entrepreneurship Competition at Northwestern University.
*   **Venture Interest**: Initiated discussions with venture firms like Y Combinator China.
